About Us

SkillHire is a skill-based job placement system designed for the Computer Science Department of CHRIST (Deemed to be University). SkillHire is aimed at helping students skilled in a particular domain to apply and be given preference for the jobs due to their expertise. The platform helps assign placements based on skills, interests, and grades through the two interfaces provided for the placement coordinator and the students. The platform SkillHire has many advantages some of which are, students skilled in a particular role have a higher chance of getting a job of their expertise, the students get notified when a career of their interest is available to apply, and the companies don't need to spend much time on filtering students who are eligible for the role.
