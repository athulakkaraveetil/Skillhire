SERVICES OFFERED

Skills Extraction

The skills of all the students are extracted by the Placement Coordinator in order to analyze the skills and interests of the students

Skill-based Job Notifications

These job options are shown on the basis on the interests and skills of the student.

All Job Notifications

The list of all the jobs that any student can apply for after looking at the role and the skillset required.

Skills Panel

This section lets the user enter their interests, marks, and certifications in order to provide specialized and specific job notifications based on the interests and credentials of the user.

Student Profile

This module will be displaying the personal information and account details of the user. It contains information that is used to identify an individual, such as their name, rollno, marks, skills, interests and individual characteristics.

Previous Applications

This interface is also designed for the user. In this interface the user will be able to view what all jobs they have applied for and the details of the job they have applied for
